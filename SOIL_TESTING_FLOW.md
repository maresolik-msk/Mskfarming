# 🌱 Soil Testing Feature - Detailed Flow

## Overview
A complete soil testing journey designed for small & marginal farmers to understand their soil health, get actionable recommendations, and improve crop yield through data-driven farming.

---

## User Journey Flow

### **Phase 1: Introduction & Awareness**
**Goal:** Help farmers understand why soil testing matters

```
Landing → Why Test? → Benefits → Get Started
```

**Screens:**
1. **Soil Health Card**
   - Shows current soil status (if tested before)
   - "Test Your Soil" CTA button
   - Visual indicator of soil health (good/moderate/poor)

2. **Why Test Soil? (Educational)**
   - 🌾 Increase crop yield by 20-30%
   - 💰 Save money on fertilizers
   - 🌍 Improve soil health over time
   - 📊 Know exactly what your soil needs
   - Voice explanation available

---

### **Phase 2: Test Selection**
**Goal:** Help farmers choose the right test for their needs

**Test Types:**

1. **Basic Test** (₹50-100)
   - NPK (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium)
   - pH Level
   - Best for: General farming
   - Duration: 3-5 days

2. **Complete Test** (₹200-300)
   - NPK + pH
   - Micronutrients (Zinc, Iron, etc.)
   - Organic Carbon
   - Soil Texture
   - Best for: High-value crops
   - Duration: 5-7 days

3. **Quick Home Test** (₹30-50)
   - DIY kit delivered to farm
   - Instant pH & NPK indicators
   - Color-coded results
   - Duration: Same day

4. **AI Photo Test** (Free - Coming Soon)
   - Take photo of soil
   - AI analyzes soil color, texture
   - Preliminary assessment
   - Duration: Instant

**Selection Screen:**
- Card layout with test options
- Price, duration, what's included
- "Recommended for you" badge
- Compare tests side-by-side

---

### **Phase 3: Sample Collection Guide**
**Goal:** Teach farmers how to collect soil samples properly

**Step-by-Step Video/Audio Guide:**

**Step 1: Timing**
- ⏰ Best time: Before planting season
- 🌧️ Avoid: Right after heavy rain
- 📅 Ideal: 2-3 weeks before sowing

**Step 2: Tools Needed**
- Khurpi (small digging tool) 🔨
- Clean bucket 🪣
- Plastic bag 🛍️
- Pen for labeling ✏️
- All items shown with images

**Step 3: Collection Points**
- Collect from 5-7 spots in field
- Avoid edges and wet areas
- Visual map showing collection pattern
- "Walk in a zig-zag" instruction

**Step 4: How to Collect**
- Dig 6 inches deep
- Remove grass/stones
- Take middle portion
- Mix all samples together

**Step 5: Prepare Sample**
- Mix well in bucket
- Take 500g from mixture
- Put in clean plastic bag
- Write: Name, Field, Date, Crop

**Interactive Features:**
- 📹 Video for each step
- 🎙️ Voice instructions
- ✅ Checklist to mark completion
- 📸 Photo examples
- ❓ "Need help?" chat button

---

### **Phase 4: Submit Sample**
**Goal:** Make sample submission easy with multiple options

**Option 1: Lab Pickup (Recommended)**
- Select pickup date & time
- Lab partner sends person to farm
- Free pickup for most areas
- Sample reaches lab same day
- Track pickup status

**Option 2: Drop at Collection Center**
- Show nearest 3 centers on map
- Get directions
- Center working hours
- Drop box available

**Option 3: Send by Post**
- Get lab address
- Packing instructions
- Tracking number entry
- Takes 2-3 extra days

**Option 4: Home Test Kit**
- Kit delivered to farm (₹50)
- DIY instructions included
- Submit results via app
- Upload photos of color strips

**Screen Elements:**
- Big cards for each option
- "Most farmers choose this" label
- Estimated time for each
- Availability in your area
- Book pickup button

**Pickup Booking:**
```
Select Date → Select Time Slot → Enter Address → 
Add Field Details → Confirm → Booking Success
```

**Confirmation Details:**
- Booking ID
- Pickup person name & photo
- Contact number
- "Track Pickup" button
- "Call Lab" button

## Key Design Principles

### 1. **Simplicity First**
- One clear action per screen
- Large buttons (min 44px)
- Simple language
- No technical jargon

### 2. **Visual Communication**
- Color codes (Green/Yellow/Red)
- Icons for every concept
- Charts and graphs
- Photos and videos

### 3. **Voice-First**
- Every screen has audio
- Voice navigation
- Listen to results
- Voice questions

### 4. **Trust Building**
- Show real lab partner
- Certified labs badge
- Success stories
- Money-back guarantee

### 5. **Offline Support**
- Download reports
- Save recommendations
- Offline tracking
- SMS notifications

### 6. **Local Language**
- Full translation
- Local terminology
- Regional crops
- Cultural context

### **State Management:**
```typescript
interface SoilTest {
  id: string;
  testType: 'basic' | 'complete' | 'home' | 'ai';
  status: 'pending' | 'collected' | 'transit' | 'testing' | 'completed';
  collectionDate: Date;
  submissionMethod: 'pickup' | 'drop' | 'post' | 'diy';
  results?: SoilTestResults;
  recommendations?: SoilRecommendations;
}

interface SoilTestResults {
  healthScore: number; // 0-100
  nitrogen: { level: number; status: 'low' | 'medium' | 'high' };
  phosphorus: { level: number; status: 'low' | 'medium' | 'high' };
  potassium: { level: number; status: 'low' | 'medium' | 'high' };
  pH: number;
  organicCarbon: number;
  soilType: 'sandy' | 'loamy' | 'clay';
  micronutrients?: Record<string, number>;
}
```
